TRS-80 Colour Computer Programmer's Handbook
This book is literally the three books TRS-80 Colour Computer Fantastic Games , TRS-80 Colour Computer Space Adventures and TRS-80 Colour Computer what Else You Can Do With Your TRS-80 bound together with a fourth section included which is a general introduction to the Color Computer included. This fourth book was published under the title "Exploring the TRS-80". These issues are literally bound together - the page numbers from the other books are included verbatim, and there are 4 tables of contents.

Radio Shack catalog # 62-8004.
